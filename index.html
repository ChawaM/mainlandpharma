Our Story

About Mainland Valley
Pharmaceutical

Founded 2024

Commitment to Innovation

Mainland Valley Pharmaceuticals Ltd was founded in 2024 as a dynamic response to the growing need for accessible, high-quality pharmaceutical products and medical equipment in Zambia.

With a deep-rooted commitment to serving the healthcare needs of our community, we embarked on this journey with a vision to redefine standards of excellence in the pharmaceutical industry.

Drawing inspiration from our parent company, Mainland Valley Investment Limited, we uphold a legacy of integrity, innovation, and a customers-first approach in every aspect of our operations.

Our Vision

To pioneer a healthcare revolution characterised by unparalleled access to innovative medicines, cutting-edge medical technologies, and holistic healthcare solutions — where every individual, irrespective of their socio-economic background, has access to safe, effective, and affordable healthcare services.

Healthcare & Beyond

We supply a comprehensive range of products serving hospitals, clinics, research labs, and the agricultural sector across Zambia.

🧪

Laboratory Consumables

  • Examination gloves (latex, nitrile, powder-free)
  • Blood collection tubes (vacutainers, serum, plasma)
  • Sterile swabs for microbiological sampling
  • Cryogenic vials for biological samples
  • Microscope slides (glass or coated)
  • Blood bags for collection and transfusion
  • HIV, Urinalysis & Pregnancy test kits
  • Cancer & Hepatitis test kits
⚕️

Medical Equipment

  • Ultrasound machines & ECG monitors
  • Infusion pumps & ventilators
  • Vital sign & patient monitoring systems
  • Anesthesia machines
  • Electrosurgical units
  • Surgical lights and theatre equipment
  • Diagnostic imaging equipment
🩺

Hospital Consumables

  • Disposable syringes and needles
  • IV sets and catheters
  • Wound dressings, bandages & sutures
  • Personal protective equipment (PPE)
  • Masks and protective gowns
  • Urinary catheters and drainage bags
💊

Medicines

  • Prescription medications (antibiotics, antihypertensives)
  • Over-the-counter medications (pain relievers, antacids)
  • Vaccines including influenza
  • Specialty biologics
  • Oncology treatments
  • Nutritional supplements
🏛️

Mortuary Equipment

  • Body bags and storage containers
  • Embalming equipment and fluids
  • Autopsy tables and instruments
  • Refrigeration units for storage
  • Protective equipment for mortuary staff
🌾

Agricultural Supplies

  • Fertilizers (organic and synthetic)
  • Pesticides and herbicides
  • High-quality certified seeds
  • Agricultural machinery
  • Soil health management products
  • Support for rural food security
